## Governance Maintenance

Governance custodianship involves regular auditing and maintenance of the governance layer:

### Responsibilities

1. **Agent Definitions** — audit agent definitions for accuracy, completeness, and alignment with current roles
2. **Rules and Knowledge** — maintain rules and knowledge artifacts, ensuring they reflect current practices
3. **Lesson Promotions** — process lessons that have reached maturity for promotion to rules
4. **Reading List Currency** — verify that knowledge references and reading lists are up to date
5. **Internal Consistency** — ensure cross-references, relationship types, and artifact status values are internally consistent

### Audit Cadence

- After every epic completion: verify no stale rules or knowledge
- After plugin updates: verify content sync integrity
- After relationship type changes: verify all existing relationships still validate
