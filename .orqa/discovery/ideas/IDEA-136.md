# IDEA-136: Legal Document Integration Plugin

A plugin inspired by [Lexicon](https://github.com/RichEsq/lexicon) that brings legal document capabilities into OrqaStudio. Contracts, compliance requirements, and legal clauses become artifacts in the graph — traceable, relationship-linked, and governed like any other artifact type.

Demonstrates OrqaStudio's domain-agnostic nature: the same governance framework that manages software delivery can manage legal documents.
