# IDEA-135: Idea Canvas

A freeform capture surface in OrqaStudio inspired by [dump.page](https://github.com/Vochsel/dump.page). Quick, unstructured thought capture that can later be promoted to structured ideas, decisions, or tasks in the artifact graph.

Bridges the gap between "I have a thought" and "I have a structured artifact" — supporting the early stages of clarity through structure without forcing structure prematurely.
