## The Idea

Once the market research agent exists (IDEA-0d7e84de), use it to research OrqaStudio's own competitive landscape:

- **AI coding assistants** — Cursor, GitHub Copilot Workspace, Windsurf, Devin, etc.
- **AI governance/structured thinking tools** — any tools that structure AI agent behavior
- **Project management + AI hybrids** — Linear + AI, Notion AI, etc.
- **Developer experience platforms** — tools focused on developer workflow orchestration

## Expected Outputs

1. A research artifact mapping the competitive landscape
2. Identified differentiators — what OrqaStudio does that competitors don't (structured governance, artifact graph, pillar-driven development, multi-agent orchestration with process enforcement)
3. Identified gaps — what competitors do well that OrqaStudio could learn from
4. Product positioning statement informed by market reality rather than assumption

## Dogfooding Value

This is a direct dogfood of the market research capability — using OrqaStudio's own agent infrastructure to research OrqaStudio's market position. If the research agent produces useful insights, that validates the capability for all software projects.
